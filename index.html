Astronomy on Tap, Graz

Welcome! This is the official website for Astronomy on Tap in Graz!

Astronomy on Tap, Graz is the first "Astronomy on Tap" event in Austria. It is a series of monthly events, where people interested in Astronomy get together in a bar and listen to nerdy talks over delicious beer. Each event will have two talks, one in English and one in German, both presented by experts in Astronomy. There's also a science-themed quiz.
