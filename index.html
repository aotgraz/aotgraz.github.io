Astronomy on Tap, Graz

Welcome! This is the official website for Astronomy on Tap in Graz!

Astronomy on Tap, Graz is the first "Astronomy on Tap" event in Austria. It is a series of monthly events, where people interested in Astronomy get together in a bar and listen to nerdy talks over delicious beer. Each event consists of two entertaining talks in English presented by experts in Astronomy. There's also a science-themed quiz and plenty of time in between to chat about space-stuff.

We are lucky to be hosted by Hops Craft Beer Pub, as they undoubtedly have the best beer selection in town!

The first event is starting on Tuesday, January 27th 2026, at 20:00. We will continue to host on every last Tuesday of the month.

To know more about what interesting topics we will cover, follow our socials here- AoT Graz :)
