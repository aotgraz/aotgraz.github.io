Kenny Goodis Gordon, PhD
Co-organizer

Hello! I'm Dr. Kenny Goodis Gordon, a postdoctoral researcher in the Exoplanets: Weather and Climate Group of the Space Research Institute (Institut für Weltraumforschung) in Graz. I work with Dr. Ludmila Carone and Dr. Christiane Helling on radiative transfer and cloud modeling of gas giant exoplanets. By simulating the transmission spectra of these worlds and comparing these simulations to observations, my work attempts to understand the 3D climate and weather development on these fascinating objects!
